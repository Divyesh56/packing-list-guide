The "5-4-3-2-1" Packing Method

If you struggle with overpacking, ditch the generic lists and use the 5-4-3-2-1 framework. It gives you exactly enough combinations for a 5-day itinerary without stuffing your suitcase:

  • 5 Sets of Basics: Underwear and socks (one for each day).
  • 4 Tops: Versatile shirts or blouses that match your bottoms.
  • 3 Bottoms: e.g., One pair of jeans, one pair of shorts, and one comfortable travel pant.
  • 2 Pairs of Shoes: One comfortable walking pair (wear these on the travel day) and one nicer pair for dinners.
  • 1 Essential Accessory: A light jacket, a sun hat, or a swimsuit.

Tailor Your List to the Destination

A 5-day getaway looks different depending on the climate. When generating your PDF on our tool, keep these quick tips in mind:

For Warm/Tropical Trips: Focus on breathable fabrics like linen or merino wool. Add a dedicated line item for reef-safe sunscreen and a dry bag.
For Cold/City Trips: You will likely wear the same heavy coat every day. Instead of packing multiple sweaters, pack thin thermal base layers to save luggage space.
